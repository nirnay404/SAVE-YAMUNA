About the Yamuna

The Yamuna River is a vital waterway for millions in India, but pollution has severely impacted its health. Our campaign focuses on awareness, clean-up efforts, and public engagement to restore the river.

Environmental Issues

Pollution from factories, domestic waste, and religious offerings contribute to the declining condition of the Yamuna. Lack of proper sewage treatment and public awareness makes it worse.

Our Actions

  • Organized Yamuna clean-up drives in Delhi
  • Awareness campaigns in schools and colleges
  • Participated in peaceful environmental protests
  • Collaborated with local NGOs and volunteers
